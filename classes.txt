Class Boat

Attributes:
boatName (string)
color (string)
materials (string)
peopleCapacity (integer)
currentPeople (array)
propulsion (string)

Methods
changeColor (modifies color attribute)
addPerson (if currentPeople < peopleCapacity then add person to currentPeople)
rename (modifies boatName attribute)
engagePropulsion (turns propulsion attribute on)
